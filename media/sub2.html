MainCHARACTER

Darth Vader

Darth Vader is considered the most dangerous and fearless among the Galactic Empire's officials. As the emperor's greatest disciple, right-hand man, closest confidant, and chief executive officer who executes the emperor's orders, he has the same political status as the next emperor of the empire.

Luke Skywalker

Luke Skywalker was a human male Force sentient who stood by Princess Leia Organa and General Han Solo and aided the Rebel Alliance in their quest to overthrow the Empire and restore freedom.

Leia Organa

The princess of Alderaan, she became a member of the Imperial Council representing Alderaan at a young age, but later became a key figure in the Rebel Alliance resisting the Galactic Empire.
