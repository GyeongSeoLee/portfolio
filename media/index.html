Evading the dreaded Imperial Starfleet, a group of freedom fighters led by Luke Skywalker has established a new secret base on the remote ice world of Hoth.

  • The evil lord Darth Vader, obsessed with finding young Skywalker, has dispatched thousands of remote probes into the far reaches of space...."The force is with you, young Skywalker. But you are not a Jedi yet."Meanwhile, Luke encounters Darth Vader in Cloud City's carbon freezing facility and engages in a lightsaber battle with him. However, Luke continues to be defeated by the skills of the powerful Sith Lord Vader, and eventually ends up cutting off the wrist holding his lightsaber.
